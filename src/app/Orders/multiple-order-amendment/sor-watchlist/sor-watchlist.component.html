Urgent SOR Watch List
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
   Note 
1. You may enter critical SOR in the watch list for close monitoring.
It will be auto-saved in database when Query/Save button is clicked.
You may omit prefix ''800...0'' when entering SOR number. To search by SO, enter full SO number.
2. To remove SOR/SO from the watch list, highlight the row(s) and click Delete button.
3. You may select multiple rows for deletion by press-and-hold (Ctrl) key (for individual selection)
or (Shift) key (for selection by range) while clicking row headers for deletion.
